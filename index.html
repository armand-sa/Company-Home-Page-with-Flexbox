Okavango Delta Conservation Alliance

Protecting and Preserving the Okavango Delta

Our Mission

The Okavango Delta Conservation Alliance is a non-profit organization dedicated to the conservation of the Okavango Delta.

Through research, education, and community engagement, we work to safeguard this vital habitat and its rich biodiversity.

Our efforts aim to balance conservation with sustainable development, ensuring a future where both wildlife and people can flourish.

The Okavango Delta Conservation Alliance is doing incredible work to protect one of the world's most unique ecosystems. Their dedication to both wildlife and local communities is truly inspiring.

— Alexandra W.

Our Conservation Efforts

Protection Programs

Initiatives to protect endangered species and combat poaching within the Delta.

Habitat Restoration

Projects aimed at restoring and preserving critical habitats within the Delta ecosystem.

Community Engagement

Working with local communities to promote sustainable practices and provide education on conservation.

Research & Monitoring

Conducting scientific research to better understand the Delta's ecosystems and inform conservation strategies.

Supporting the Alliance has been a fulfilling experience. Their innovative approaches to conservation are making a significant impact on the Delta's diverse wildlife and habitats.

— Richard T.
